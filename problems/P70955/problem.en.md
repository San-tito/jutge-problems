# How many seconds are they?

Write a program that converts to seconds a given amount of years, days,
hours, minutes and seconds.

## Input

The input consists of five natural numbers that represent the years,
days, hours, minutes and seconds, respectively.

## Output

Write the total number of seconds represented by the input.

## Observation

You may assume all the years have 365 days.
