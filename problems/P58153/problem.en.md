# Harmonic numbers (2)

Write a program that reads pairs of numbers $n$ and $m$ with $n \ge m$,
and for each pair prints $H_n - H_m$.

## Input

Input consists of several pairs of natural numbers $n$ and $m$ such that
$n \ge m$.

## Output

For every pair, print $H_n - H_m$ with 10 digits after the decimal
point.
